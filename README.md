Website for the band: Velozza

Velozza is a thrash metal band from Roermond, the Netherlands, formed in 2016. The four members — Bas (lead guitar), Rob (drums), Jos (guitar & vocals), and Ron (bass) — deliver raw, uncompromising thrash that channels the spirit of the ’80s while carving out a sound of their own.

Prepare for an explosive onslaught of metal power! For fans of Slayer, Kreator, Havok. This quad delivers high- speed, heart-pounding songs and crushing, heavy anthems that leave metalheads breathless. Join the mosh pit and unleash your inner metal warrior as Velozza takes you on an thrashing journey, conquering the world one show at a time.
